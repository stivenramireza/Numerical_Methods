HELP Relaxed Jacobi Method!

This method is an extension of the fixed point method. The system Ax = b
is transformed into the system x = Tx + C. A vector x1 is found from a 
vector x0. With x1, x2 is calculated and so on until a certain tolerance is met.
This presents a relaxed <1 or >1.
