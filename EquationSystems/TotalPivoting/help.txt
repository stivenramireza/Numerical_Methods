HELP Total Pivoting Method!

This method exchanges rows and columns of the matrix A looking for the largest element,
in absolute value, on the diagonal A [k] [k] and in this way obtain small multipliers
with large pivots. Simple Gaussian elimination is applied and the system of equations is solved.
